Runing is one of the hottest fitness trends around-again. Because it's fun, easy and cheap. Here is a tone of great running tips to keep you compfy, safe and happy!

1. Make sure you're really ready to start running if you're new to it.

Alternate running and walking for 45 minutes to an hour every day for two weeks, gradually easing yourself into daily jogs.

2. Buy the right running shoe size.

Your foot should fits snug in the heel, and you should have the space to wiggle your toes.

3. Do some yoga or stretch before and after you run.

It helps you to prevent some injuries. This video teaches you to do it easily.
